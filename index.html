AI PING PONG GAME









Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move about 3 or 4 feet away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist up and down, a red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. Now press the play button, and still stay 3 or 4 feet away from laptop
  5. The red paddle is yours, it will move based on the movement of your right wrist
